# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want `imuops` to be a respectful, technically honest, and low-drama project.

Examples of expected behavior:

- assume good intent and communicate clearly
- criticize ideas and implementations, not people
- share reproducible evidence when raising issues
- use accurate wording about what the tool does and does not guarantee

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- deliberately misleading claims about results or support status
- publishing private data from real users or partners without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this standard.

## Scope

This applies to issues, discussions, pull requests, and project communication channels.
